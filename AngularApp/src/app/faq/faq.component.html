Frequently Asked Questions

  • What is an 'unknown' game?
    There's two reasons a game is marked as unknown:
    1. The game has been deleted by Steam
    2. The game has not yet been scraped to our database (usually for new games)
  • Why can't I see my games when I search for my profile?
    Your profile may be private or you may have entered your Steam ID or profile URL incorrectly. To find your profile URL, go to your steam profile in browser and copy-paste the url to our search bar.
  • Is Condensate Open Source?
    Yes, see the about page for details.
  • How can I hide free / unknown / unplayed games?
    After finding your profile, click on the green buttons below the search bar to hide free / unknown / unplayed games.
  • Is there a way to export my profile information?
    There is currently no way to export your data, but could be a future feature.
  • Is there a way to share my profile information?
    Copy the link after searching for a URL and you can give that link to your friends.
